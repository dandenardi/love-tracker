# Child Safety Standards — Love Tracker

**Last Updated: May 5, 2026**

Love Tracker is committed to maintaining a safe environment and protecting children from any form of abuse or exploitation. As a private relationship journal designed for consenting adults in a committed partnership, we take child safety seriously.

## 1. Zero Tolerance Policy

Love Tracker has a zero-tolerance policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and any content related to the abuse or exploitation of minors (CSAE). We comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including reporting illegal activities to the appropriate authorities.

## 2. Private-By-Design Architecture

To prevent misuse and ensure safety, Love Tracker is built with a private-by-design approach:

- **No Public Discovery**: There are no public profiles, search functions, or discovery features.
- **Invitation-Only Pairing**: Users can only connect with a specific partner using a unique, one-time invitation code generated locally and shared privately.
- **Private Data**: Personal logs marked as "Private" never leave the user's device.

## 3. Prohibited Content

Users are strictly prohibited from using Love Tracker to store, share, or transmit any illegal content, including but not limited to:

- Content depicting or promoting the sexual abuse or exploitation of minors.
- Content used to groom or facilitate the exploitation of children.

## 4. Reporting and Enforcement

If any user is found to be using the platform for illegal activities related to child safety:

- Their account will be permanently banned.
- All associated data on our servers will be deleted (after coordination with authorities if required).
- We will report the incident to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or other relevant international bodies.

## 5. In-App Reporting Tools
To satisfy safety requirements, Love Tracker provides a direct way for users to report safety concerns:
- **Report Button**: Users can navigate to **Settings > Support & Safety** and tap **Report a Safety Concern**. This allows users to instantly report any suspected illegal activity or child safety issues directly to our team.
- **Immediate Action**: All reports received via the in-app tool are prioritized for review.
